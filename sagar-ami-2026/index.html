The Experience

About This Trip

This is a 12-day journey across both islands of New Zealand, built around the kind of experiences we genuinely love. From Māori culture and glowworm rafting in Rotorua to lakeside sauna, group picnics, and slow scenic moments in the South Island, it’s a mix of adventure and easy, feel-good travel.

There’s also space to level it up with options like a heli ride in Mount Cook, bungee in Queenstown, or skydiving in Wanaka. It’s not rushed, not touristy, just a well-paced trip that lets you actually enjoy New Zealand the way we do.

Need To Know

FAQs

A few helpful answers before you book. If you’re still unsure about anything, just message us and we’ll talk it through.

Can we bring kids? +
For our first few trips, we’re keeping it adults-only so we can create a smooth, flexible experience for everyone. We’d love to explore family-friendly trips in the future once we’ve done a few of these!
Do we need to be super fit or experienced travellers? +
Not at all. These trips are designed to be fun and manageable, even if you don’t travel often. We’ll always share the activity level upfront so you know exactly what to expect.
How many people will be on the trip? +
We’re keeping the groups small and personal (10 people) so it doesn’t feel like a tour bus. Think a close-knit group where you actually get to know everyone.
Can I come solo? +
100%. A lot of people join these trips solo, and that’s kind of the best part. You leave with new friends. We’ll make sure it feels easy and welcoming from day one.
How does payment work? +
To secure your spot, we require a deposit upfront, with the remaining balance split into easy instalments. We also offer flexible payment options like Afterpay if that works better for you.
What if I need to cancel? +
Plans change, we get it. You can cancel up to 60 days before the trip, and after that a portion of the payment becomes non-refundable, full details will be shared clearly before booking.
Are all meals included? +
We’ve included 2 group dinners and 1 lunch. Breakfast and the rest of the meals are not included, so you have the flexibility to explore at your own pace.
