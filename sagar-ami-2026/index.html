The Experience

About This Trip

This is a 12-day journey across both islands of New Zealand, built around the kind of experiences we genuinely love. From Māori culture and glowworm cave rafting in Rotorua to lakeside sauna, group picnics, and slow scenic moments in the South Island — it's a mix of adventure and easy, feel-good travel.

There's also space to level it up with options like a helicopter ride over Mount Cook, bungee jumping in Queenstown, or skydiving in Wanaka. It's not rushed, not touristy — just a well-paced trip that lets you actually enjoy New Zealand the way we do.

"Not rushed, not touristy — just New Zealand the way it should be."

Meet Sagar & Ami

Doubtful Sound · New Zealand

Indian-born Kiwis,
living slow.

We're Sagar and Ami — Indian-born Kiwis who've been living and travelling across New Zealand for the last 10 years.

Two years ago, we left our jobs to travel full-time around the world. Now we're back living in our van, exploring NZ slowly while building a life around travel.

We love simple moments, real experiences, and showing a side of travel that feels honest and doable.

This is our very first group trip — so it's a special one, and we'd love for you to be part of it.

— Sagar & Ami
